Indoor Gaming Activities

We recognize the power of games in fostering teamwork, critical thinking, and problem-solving skills. Our indoor gaming sessions provide a fun and interactive way for members to relax while sharpening their minds.

Popular Games We Play:

  • Chess - A strategy game that improves critical thinking and decision-making.
  • Scrabble - A word-based game that enhances vocabulary and creativity.
  • Darts - A game of precision, patience, and focus.
  • Card Games - From Uno to poker, card games teach strategy and quick decision-making.
  • Board Games - Games like Monopoly and Ludo encourage financial literacy and social interaction.
  • Table Tennis - A fast-paced game that improves reflexes and hand-eye coordination.

We organize gaming tournaments every Saturday afternoon, where participants compete for fun and exciting prizes!

Weekly Meetings & Networking

In addition to gaming, we hold weekly meetings designed to empower members, discuss opportunities, and plan for the club’s growth.

Virtual Meetings:

Every Wednesday at 7:00 PM, we connect via Google Meet or Zoom to discuss upcoming activities, share insights, and motivate each other.

Physical Meetings:

We meet every Saturday at 2:00 PM at Kiambu National Polytechnic, providing a platform for interactive discussions, networking, and skill development.

Topics discussed during these meetings include:

  • Financial literacy and investment opportunities
  • Technology trends and innovations
  • Freelancing and online job opportunities
  • Personal development and mindset growth

Prize Pool for Gaming Champions

To make our gaming competitions more exciting, we offer a prize pool for top performers:

  • 1st Place - KES 500
  • 2nd Place - KES 300
  • 3rd Place - KES 200

The winners are announced at the end of each month, and the leaderboard is updated on our notice board.
